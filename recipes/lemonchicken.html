lemon chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 large boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Flour
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice + zest of 1/2 lemon

Steps

  1. Cut your chicken breasts in half lengthwise so you have thinner cutlets. Sprinkle them with salt & pepper and the garlic powder. Dredge them in flour so they're nicely coated.
  2. Add the olive oil and 1 tablespoon of the butter to a skillet over medium-high heat. Once the pan is hot, add the chicken cutlets and cook them for 4-5 minutes/side or until theyâ€™re nice and golden. Take them out of the pan and set aside.
  3. Add the chicken broth, lemon juice + zest, and remaining butter to the pan and let it bubble for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add the chicken back in and cook for another few minutes until it's cooked through and the sauce is further reduced. Season with salt & pepper as needed and sprinkle the fresh parsley over top if using.
